Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Tabriz rug is a refined example of figurative design, featuring an artistic composition rich in detail and symbolism. The central field showcases stylized birds, reminiscent of mythical creatures such as the phoenix, symbolizing rebirth and harmony. Floral and vegetal motifs intertwine to create a balanced and elegant visual composition. The oval central field stands out against a lighter background, framed by a finely decorated floral border. The soft and pastel color palette enhances the overall elegance and brightness of the rug. Hand-knotted with great precision, this rug is ideal as a decorative or collectible piece, perfect for refined and elegant interiors.
