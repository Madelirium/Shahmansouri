Details A carefully selected piece.

This handmade Caucasian Baku rug perfectly reflects the decorative tradition of Caucasian weaving, renowned for its bold geometric motifs and vibrant color combinations. The central field features a refined symmetrical composition of stylized tribal symbols and geometric floral elements in shades of red, blue, and ivory. The detailed borders frame the design beautifully, giving the rug strong decorative character and visual balance. Handcrafted using traditional techniques, this rug is ideal for classic, rustic, or ethnic interiors, while its graphic tribal patterns can also create striking contrasts in contemporary spaces.
