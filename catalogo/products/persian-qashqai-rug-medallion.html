Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Qashqai rug is an authentic example of tribal craftsmanship, hand-knotted by nomadic populations of southern Iran. The design is defined by a strong central diamond-shaped medallion, surrounded by multiple layered borders filled with geometric and stylized floral motifs. The composition is balanced and reflects the distinctive visual identity of Qashqai rugs. The deep red background is enriched with dark blue, beige, and lighter accents, creating a harmonious yet striking contrast. The warm tones make the rug highly decorative and adaptable to different interior styles. The border consists of multiple bands with traditional motifs that frame the rug and enhance its overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for everyday use, ideal for living rooms, entrances, or study areas. A unique piece combining tradition, strength, and aesthetic value.
