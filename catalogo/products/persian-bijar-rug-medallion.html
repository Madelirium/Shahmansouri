Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Bijar rug is an authentic example of traditional craftsmanship from western Iran, renowned for its exceptional durability and dense structure. The design is defined by an elongated central medallion set within a richly detailed geometric composition. Stylized motifs inspired by nature and local traditions are distributed harmoniously across the surface. The background combines deep red and dark blue tones, enhanced by lighter accents that create a bold yet balanced contrast, typical of Bijar rugs. The border is composed of multiple bands with traditional motifs that frame the rug and reinforce its visual structure. Hand-knotted in high-quality wool, this rug is extremely durable, often referred to as the “iron rug” due to its compact weave. Ideal for high-traffic areas such as living rooms and entrances. A unique piece combining strength, tradition, and aesthetic value.
