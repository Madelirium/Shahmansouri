Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Malayer rug is an authentic example of traditional craftsmanship from western Iran, known for its combination of geometric patterns and tribal influences. The design is defined by a central medallion set within a richly decorated field of stylized geometric and floral motifs. The composition is balanced and harmonious, reflecting the typical visual identity of Malayer rugs. The deep red background is enhanced by dark blue, beige, and lighter accents, adding depth and movement while maintaining a warm and elegant appearance. The border is decorated with continuous traditional motifs that frame the rug and enhance its overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for everyday use, ideal for living rooms, entrances, or study areas. A unique piece combining tradition, strength, and aesthetic value.
