Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Bakhtiari rug represents a more refined interpretation of tribal tradition, with stylistic influences closer to classical Persian carpets. The design is dominated by an elegant central medallion, surrounded by dense floral and arabesque motifs that spread harmoniously across the entire field. The composition is rich yet well balanced, with fine details that highlight the quality of craftsmanship. The deep red background is enhanced by dark blue, ivory, and lighter accents that add depth and movement, giving the rug a warm and sophisticated appearance. The border is finely decorated with continuous floral motifs that frame the rug and enhance its overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for elegant interiors such as living rooms, dining rooms, or studies. A unique piece combining tradition and refined aesthetics.
