Details A carefully selected piece.

This refined Nain 6La Persian rug represents one of the most elegant and prestigious expressions of Persian weaving tradition. Finely hand-knotted with an exceptionally harmonious design, the rug features a sophisticated floral central medallion surrounded by delicate arabesque motifs. The soft shades of light blue, ivory, and beige create a luminous and balanced composition, ideal for elegant and sophisticated interiors. The finely executed floral decoration reflects the artistic tradition of renowned Nain rugs, celebrated worldwide for their precision and refined craftsmanship. The term “6La” identifies a particularly fine quality within Nain rug production, known for its higher knot density, intricate decorative details, and refined weaving structure. Nain rugs are among the most appreciated Persian carpets thanks to their elegance, floral artistry, and harmonious color palettes. Thanks to its versatile size, this rug is perfect for a study, entrance, bedroom, or refined smaller interior spaces where it can add timeless Persian elegance.
