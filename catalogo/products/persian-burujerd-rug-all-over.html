Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Burujerd rug is an authentic example of traditional craftsmanship from western Iran, known for its repeating patterns and durability. The rug features an all-over composition with a harmonious repetition of geometric motifs and stylized tribal symbols across the entire surface. The absence of a central medallion creates a continuous and dynamic visual effect, typical of Burujerd rugs. The dark background, in shades of deep blue and black, is enriched with decorative elements in red, beige, and light blue tones, offering a balanced and elegant contrast. The border is decorated with traditional motifs that frame the rug and enhance its overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for everyday use, ideal for living rooms, entrances, or study areas. A unique piece combining strength, tradition, and visual character.
