Details A carefully selected piece.

This vintage Persian Baktiari khorjin is a traditional double saddlebag with a strong tribal character. The two woven panels show central medallions, geometric motifs and detailed borders, with deep dark tones, warm reds, rich blues and lighter accents that bring rhythm to the composition. Originally made as a practical textile object, this khorjin is now appreciated as a decorative and collectible Persian textile.
