Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Hosseinabad rug is a refined example of traditional craftsmanship from western Iran, known for its balanced designs and decorative versatility. The rug features a large central medallion surrounded by finely stylized floral and arabesque motifs. The composition is harmonious and well-structured, with details that fill the space elegantly without overwhelming it. The deep red background is enhanced by ivory and dark blue accents, creating a refined contrast that adds depth and highlights the design. The border is decorated with continuous floral patterns that frame the rug and complete its classic appearance. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and ideal for elegant interiors such as living rooms, dining rooms, or studies. A unique piece combining tradition, elegance, and functionality.
