Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Bakhtiari rug is an authentic example of tribal craftsmanship, hand-knotted by nomadic populations of southwestern Iran. The design is defined by a large elongated central medallion that develops vertically across a structured geometric field. Within the field, stylized floral motifs and traditional symbols reflect the culture and natural inspiration of the Bakhtiari tribes. The color palette is warm and balanced, featuring rust red, beige, and deep blue tones, enhanced by green accents that add depth and contrast. The border is richly decorated with repeating geometric and floral motifs that frame the rug and emphasize its overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for everyday use, making it ideal for living rooms, entrances, or study areas. A unique piece combining tradition, function, and decorative value.
