Details A carefully selected piece.

This Turkmen Persian rug stands out for its refined contrast between the bright ivory background and the traditional red geometric motifs inspired by Central Asian nomadic art. Its symmetrical and structured design reflects classic Turkmen tribal patterns, reinterpreted in an elegant and luminous composition. The geometric borders frame the central field harmoniously, creating a decorative effect that feels both traditional and modern. Hand-knotted using traditional craftsmanship, this rug is ideal for bright, contemporary, or minimalist interiors where it can add warmth and character without overwhelming the space.
