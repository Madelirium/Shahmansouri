Details A carefully selected piece.

This refined Qom Persian rug stands out for its exceptional decorative elegance and the richness of its floral details that gracefully cover the entire composition. The deep midnight blue background beautifully enhances the ivory and red central medallion along with the delicate floral motifs spread harmoniously across the field. The richly decorated border, filled with elegant scrolling vines and stylized floral ornaments, reflects the artistic tradition of renowned Qom rugs, famous for their fine craftsmanship and sophisticated designs. The combination of blue, red, ivory, and green tones creates an elegant and timeless visual balance suitable for classic and refined interiors. Qom rugs are highly appreciated for their precision, decorative harmony, and fine weaving quality. This piece features an elegant floral composition with strong visual impact, ideal for entrances, studies, bedrooms, or refined living spaces. Handmade according to Persian weaving tradition, this rug represents a beautiful example of Oriental textile artistry combining elegance, craftsmanship, and decorative sophistication.
