Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Bakhtiari rug is an authentic example of tribal craftsmanship, hand-knotted by nomadic populations of southwestern Iran. The design features a complex “garden” structure, divided into geometric compartments filled with a rich variety of decorative elements. Within these sections, stylized trees, flowers, animals, and traditional symbols reflect nature and nomadic life. The composition is intricate yet balanced, with a symmetrical arrangement that creates a harmonious and visually rich design. The light background, in shades of beige and ivory, contrasts beautifully with the warm tones of red and orange and the darker hues of blue and black, enhancing every decorative detail. The border is richly decorated with traditional motifs that frame the rug and strengthen its overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for everyday use, ideal for living rooms, entrances, or study areas. A unique piece combining tradition, symbolism, and aesthetic value.
