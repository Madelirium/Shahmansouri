Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Baktiari rug is an authentic example of tribal craftsmanship, hand-knotted by nomadic populations of southwestern Iran. The design features the typical “garden” structure, divided into geometric compartments filled with rich decorative elements. Within these sections, floral motifs, stylized trees, animals, and traditional symbols reflect nature and nomadic culture. The composition is complex yet balanced, with a symmetrical layout that creates a harmonious and visually engaging design. The color palette is vibrant and contrasting, with tones of red, black, beige, and accents of green and light blue, adding depth and character. The border is richly decorated with traditional motifs that frame the central field and enhance the overall structure. Hand-knotted in quality wool, this rug is durable and suitable for everyday use, ideal for living rooms, entrances, or study areas. A unique piece combining tradition, symbolism, and decorative value.
