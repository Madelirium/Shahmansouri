Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian ArmaniBaf rug is an authentic example of tribal craftsmanship from Iran, characterized by a simple yet meaningful symbolic style. The design features a central composition of stylized geometric motifs and tribal figures, arranged in a spontaneous yet harmonious way. These elements reflect nomadic culture and everyday life, making each rug unique. The color palette is warm and natural, with shades of brown, beige, antique pink, and darker accents that add depth and a charming aged look. The border is decorated with simple traditional motifs that frame the rug and enhance its overall structure. Hand-knotted in wool, this rug is durable and perfect for cozy, rustic, or ethnic interiors. A unique piece combining simplicity, tradition, and character.
