Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Senneh rug is an elegant example of craftsmanship from Iranian Kurdistan, valued for its precise design and fine knotting. The rug features a well-defined geometric central medallion surrounded by a dense field of stylized motifs arranged with balance and symmetry. The composition is compact and highly detailed, typical of small-size Senneh rugs. The dark background enhances the light medallion, creating a strong and elegant contrast, while red, pink, and blue accents add depth and vibrancy. The border is decorated with regular geometric motifs that frame the rug and highlight its fine craftsmanship. Hand-knotted in wool, this rug is durable and ideal for smaller spaces such as entrances, studies, or as a decorative piece. A unique example combining elegance, precision, and tradition.
