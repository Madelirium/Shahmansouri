Details A carefully selected piece.

This signed Persian Isfahan rug measures 130x85 cm and shows a fine handmade structure with silk warp and weft. The pile is mixed with silk, with a silk field that gives the surface a soft and delicate light. The pale central field holds a rich floral medallion, framed by a refined border in antique rose, ivory, light blue and brown tones. An elegant and balanced small-format Isfahan, suitable for those looking for a signed Persian rug with fine workmanship.
