Details A carefully selected piece.

This signed Persian Isfahan rug measures 105x75 cm and shows a refined handmade structure, with silk warp and weft. The pile is mixed with silk, with a strong presence of silk on the surface, visible in the light and soft reflections of the cream field. The design is decorated with repeated boteh motifs, arranged with balance and framed by a floral border in beige, gold, rose and brown tones. An elegant small-format Persian rug, suitable for those looking for a refined decorative piece with a delicate and luminous character.
