Details A carefully selected piece.

This antique handmade Caucasian Daghistan rug reflects the authentic weaving tradition of the Caucasus, renowned for its bold geometric patterns and strong tribal symbolism. The elongated central field is decorated with large diamond-shaped motifs arranged in sequence, woven in shades of red, ivory, and beige that create a striking decorative presence and harmonious visual balance. The geometric borders complete the composition with elegance and traditional craftsmanship. Its narrow elongated format makes this rug particularly suitable for hallways, entrances, or interiors where an antique textile with strong aesthetic and cultural value is desired. The natural wear and aged patina enhance the authentic charm of the piece.
