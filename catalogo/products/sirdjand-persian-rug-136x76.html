Details A carefully selected piece.

This handmade Sirdjand Persian rug features an elegant geometric composition with two large central medallions inspired by traditional Persian tribal designs. The deep shades of blue, brown, and ivory create a striking decorative contrast, while the zigzag patterns and geometric details reflect the ancient nomadic culture of Iran. Its symmetrical layout gives the rug strong visual balance and character. Handcrafted using traditional weaving techniques, this rug is ideal for classic, rustic, or ethnic interiors where it can become a refined decorative centerpiece full of authenticity and history.
