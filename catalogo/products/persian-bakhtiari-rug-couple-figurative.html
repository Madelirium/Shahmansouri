Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Bakhtiari rug is a refined example of figurative design, featuring a romantic and expressive scene. At the center, a couple in traditional attire is depicted, surrounded by floral motifs that enhance the elegance and symbolic meaning of the composition. The oval frame creates a strong visual impact, while the red background adds warmth and depth. The details are finely executed, highlighting facial expressions, garments, and decorative elements. This type of rug stands out not only for its craftsmanship but also for its artistic value. Ideal as a decorative or wall rug, it is perfect for those looking for a unique piece that tells a story and enriches any interior.
