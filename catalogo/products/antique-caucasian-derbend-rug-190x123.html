Details A carefully selected piece.

This antique handmade Caucasian Derbend rug is an elegant example of Caucasian weaving tradition, renowned for its symbolic geometric motifs and richly balanced decorative compositions. The central design features a stylized geometric structure filled with tribal elements and ornamental symbols arranged harmoniously throughout the field. The delicate shades of pink, ivory, and blue give the rug a refined and luminous appearance rarely found in traditional Caucasian pieces. Decorative borders complete the composition with repeating motifs typical of Caucasian culture, while the handcrafted weaving and aged patina enhance the rug’s authentic charm. Ideal for elegant interiors, antique rug collections, or spaces seeking a textile rich in history and personality.
