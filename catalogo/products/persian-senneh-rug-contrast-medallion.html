Details A carefully selected piece.

This Persian Senneh rug is a refined example of craftsmanship from Iranian Kurdistan, appreciated for its precision and clarity of design. The rug features a well-defined central medallion composed of layered geometric forms that create depth and visual movement. The structure is symmetrical and balanced, with stylized motifs arranged in an orderly pattern. The contrast between the dark outer field and the lighter central area enhances the medallion, creating an elegant and striking visual effect. Shades of red, blue, and ivory blend harmoniously, highlighting the intricate details. The border is decorated with small, regular motifs typical of Senneh rugs, emphasizing the fine craftsmanship and framing the composition. Hand-knotted in wool, this rug is durable and ideal for smaller interiors such as entrances, studies, or refined spaces. A unique piece combining precision, contrast, and tradition.
