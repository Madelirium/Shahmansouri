Shahmansouri Persian Carpets in Verona

Persian carpets, Persian handicrafts, carpet washing and restoration in the historic centre of Verona

MehdiShahmansouri, originally from the province of Isfahan (Iran), comes from a family of wholesale merchants for several generations and lives and works in Verona where since 1991, as a direct importer, he also offers retail services in an exclusive and prestigious location within the ancient city near the Cathedral.

Shahmansouri is not the typical carpet store but stands out for originality and style. Presenting many aspects of Persian art: fine and antique carpets, modern and old knotted pieces. Unique pieces for quality and originality accompany tribal artefacts, Kilim, sacks, bags, Korjin, Jajim, Javal and cushions.

He exclusively proposes Persian handicraft: such as embossed vases, blown glass, ceramics, hand-printed fabrics, miniatures, inlay work, frames and objects. All authentic and refined objects rigorously handmade.

The seriousness and competence shown by this long-standing business, with a stable base in Verona, guarantees a solid relationship that does not end with the purchase, but will be the basis of a future relationship since the carpet requires washing, maintenance and custody, or even simple advice.

The policy of Shahmansouri lies in gaining the customer's trust by offering availability and services, including home staging and accurate washing and restoration.

It is worth noting the "Wedding List" initiative organized for young couples who appreciate the carpet as an exclusive gift.
