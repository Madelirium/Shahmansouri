Where we are

Our shop is located in the heart of Verona, at Stradone Arcidiacono Pacifico 14. You can find us in the historic centre, easily reachable on foot or by car. Here is a simple summary to help you reach us.

Verona: a European city

Verona is a UNESCO World Heritage city and one of the most fascinating art cities in Europe. Famous for the Arena, Romeo and Juliet, and its food-and-wine tradition, it offers an ideal setting for our carpets. A visit to our shop can also become an opportunity to discover a historic centre rich in monuments, piazzas and traditional restaurants.
